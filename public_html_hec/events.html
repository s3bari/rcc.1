Special Events

 

30 March, 2006
By the end of May the HEC is hoping to send three of its members along with Xof1 to break a world record for the longest distance traveled by a solar car.  The students that will be making this journey include Shane Harris, Michael Hall, and Bryan Sturrman.  The trip will take them right across Canada from coast to coast twice totaling a distance of 22000 KM. For more information on the solar car visit the Xof1 solar car page.
