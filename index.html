The club initially known as Hobby Computer Club was officially established in October 2006 with the aim to apply theoretical engineering knowledge. As it evolves it aims to teaching computing technologies such as chip design, programming and networking.

The HCC offers a scope for all to apply and further their skills, learn about new technologies and network with other students who share similar interests. Furthermore HCC looks forward to involving itself in the community and inspiring engineers and engineering. Contact us to join and find your place at the HCC.
